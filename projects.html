Vishwakarma University Cafeteria App (launching soon)

"Smart, simple, and seamless food ordering for the Vishwakarma University community."

The Vishwakarma University Cafeteria App is a dedicated mobile platform designed to simplify food ordering and management within the campus. Students can explore the cafeteria menu, place and track orders, reorder favorites, and manage their profiles with ease.

On the other hand, cafeteria owners gain access to a complete dashboard where they can update menus, monitor and manage incoming orders in real-time, accept or reject requests, and review completed sales.

With built-in language support for English, Hindi, and Marathi, the app ensures accessibility for a diverse student community, while streamlining cafeteria operations and enhancing the overall dining experience on campus.

Key Features

  • Menu exploration and ordering
  • Order tracking and history
  • Multi-language support
  • Admin dashboard for cafeteria management
  • Real-time order notifications
